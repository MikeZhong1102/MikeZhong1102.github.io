Coaster

by Mike Zhong

This is my first project in my MAT 111 PF class. This is a coaster made of wood by laser-cutting mathine. I designed the textures on Adobe illustrator, then exported them as svg files, so the mathine knew where to cut and where to engrave.



Bookshelf

by Mike Zhong

This is my second project in my MAT 111 PF class. This is also made of wood by laser-cutting mathine. I used half-lap joints principle to make all the parts fit together.



Phone Case

by Mike Zhong

This is my final project in my MAT 111 PF class. This is a phone case made of pla by 3D printer. I made the model by blender and then gave it to the 3D printer then printed all the parts, then assemble them by hands.
